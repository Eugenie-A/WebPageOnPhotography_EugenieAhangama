My Passion for Photography

Photography - My Greatest Passion

Photography is not just a hobby for me. It has become a way of seeing the world. Ever since I got my iPhone 15 Pro Max and Canon EOS R10 mirrorless camera with the RF-S 18-150mm f/3.5-6.3 IS STM lens in 2024, I've been chasing emotions and moments through a lens. Whether it's golden hour landscapes, street scenes full of life, flowers in my garden, or intimate portraits of friendly and family, every click teaches me about storytelling, patience and composition.

I love experimenting with different genres: long-exposure night shots, macro photography of flowers and insects, minimalist architecture, and candid travel photography.

Editing in Lightroom and occasionally Photoshop is the final magical step that turns a good photo into something I'm truly proud of.

Why I Love Photography

  • Lets me be creative every single day
  • I can share emotions without saying a word
  • It freezes a plit-second of life forever
  • Seeing the world differently
  • Stress relief and Mindfulness
  • Leaving a legacy
